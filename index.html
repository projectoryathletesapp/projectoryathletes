Questions?

Is Projectory free?

100% free forever. No premium tiers, no paywalls, no hidden fees. It's built for athletes, not for profit.

What are the founding member perks?

Founding members get: early access 30 days before launch, "Founding Member" badge forever, exclusive Discord community, and priority support. The app is completely free for everyone.

Can I post training videos?

Yes. Post 15-second clips to showcase your skill level. Videos help you get matched with athletes at your tier.

What if I feel unsafe?

Block instantly. Report any behavior with one tap. Our team reviews reports within 24 hours. Zero tolerance for abuse.

When does it launch?

September 1, 2026 on the App Store. Founding members get access 30 days earlier (August 1, 2026).

What sports are supported?

All NCAA, NAIA, club, and intramural sports. Soccer, basketball, lacrosse, swimming, track, volleyball, tennis, rowing, football, baseball, softball, golf, and more.

Can I delete my account?

Yes. Delete anytime in settings. All data removed within 30 days. No questions asked. No data sold or retained.

Is my data safe?

Yes. Firebase security, end-to-end encryption, GDPR/CCPA compliant. Your data is never shared or sold.
